Simon Knoz

I am the Freelance Photographer. I usually take pictures of the view, scenes and nature. Born in Myanmar, grew up in Myanmar, Burma. I travel most of the places in Myanmar often. My Goal is to show the beauty, picturesque, nature & enthusiastic of MYANMAR. I focus on non-models and place regular people in exaggerated situations that highlight an aspect of who they are. I look for a sense of defiance and fortitude in my work. I'm based in Mandalay but travel for photo-shoots around MYANMAR.
